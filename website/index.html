Why VaultDrop exists

A better way to capture, organize, and revisit saved media links.

VaultDrop turns shared links into a structured local library. Instead of losing track of reels, posts, and videos, you can download them, save them as bookmarks, add reusable tags, and come back later with thumbnails, notes, and a clean timeline view.

Feature walkthrough

How people actually use VaultDrop

See UI screenshots
01

Share into VaultDrop

Tap Share from Instagram or YouTube and VaultDrop appears as a share target. The app detects the platform and prepares either a download action or a bookmark save flow.

02

Choose what to do

Send the link to the download queue, or save it to Bookmarks with a note. On the save flow, you can tap reusable tags before writing the final comment.

03

Track downloads

Open Downloads to watch active items, review progress, and inspect queued or failed jobs. Each download card shows metadata and visual status feedback.

04

Browse the library

Completed files are collected in Library, where you can open the built-in player, browse by date, and access content quickly in a visual grid.

05

Bookmark with context

Saved links appear in a thumbnail-first Vault view. Long press any card to preview, open in browser, copy the link, refresh the thumbnail, or delete the bookmark.

06

Repair and refine

Settings includes tools to repair Instagram URLs, refresh thumbnails, manage session login, and maintain reusable bookmark tags without leaving the app.

How it works

From share sheet to saved media

1. Shared link arrives

The app receives an Instagram or YouTube text link through the Android share sheet and detects the platform automatically.

2. Downloader resolves media

VaultDrop fetches metadata, thumbnails, and media URLs, then sends downloads to the queue if that path is chosen.

3. Bookmark gets enriched

Saved links receive thumbnail previews, optional tags, comments, and cleaned canonical URLs for long-term use.

4. Settings maintain the library

If thumbnails or URLs degrade, Settings can repair Instagram links, refresh previews, and manage your login session.
